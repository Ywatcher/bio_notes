# Source Notes

## Starting Point

- Claverie and Langman 1984: original local PDF. Used to understand the stochastic/computer model and the proposed feedback stop condition.

## Reviews / Model Context

- Vettermann and Schlissel 2010: review of immunoglobulin allelic exclusion models and mechanisms.
- Peng et al. 2026: review on dual-receptor lymphocytes and incomplete allelic exclusion; useful as a recent broad pointer, not a core mechanism source.

## Receptor Signaling and Allelic Exclusion

- Iritani et al. 1999: separates maturation/expansion signals from allelic exclusion signals.
- Wen et al. 2004: PLCgamma1 role in pre-BCR signaling and heavy-chain allelic exclusion.

## RAG / FOXO1 / PI3K-AKT Feedback

- Amin and Schlissel 2008: FOXO1 directly regulates Rag1/Rag2 transcription.
- Dengler et al. 2008: FOXO1 functions at multiple B-cell developmental stages, including Rag expression.
- Lazorchak et al. 2010: mTORC2-AKT2 suppresses Rag and Il7r via FOXO1.
- Novak et al. 2010: MAPK/ERK and PI3K coordinate Rag transcription.
- Schulz et al. 2012: Gfi1b represses Rag directly and through Foxo1.
- Coffre et al. 2016: miRNAs regulate PTEN-AKT-FOXO1 and receptor editing.
- Benhamou et al. 2018: c-Myc/miR17-92/PTEN tunes PI3K activity to control Rag expression.

## DNA-Damage Feedback

- Bednarski et al. 2016: RAG-mediated DNA breaks activate ATM/NF-kB2/SPIC checkpoint feedback that inhibits pre-BCR signaling.
- Ochodnicka-Mackovicova et al. 2016: DNA damage downregulates RAG1/2 through ATM-FOXO1 signaling.

## Chromatin / Locus Architecture / Receptor Editing

- Mandal et al. 2015: BRWD1 targets and restricts recombination to Igk.
- Hill et al. 2023: Igh and Igk use different folding principles during V gene recombination.
- Li et al. 2026: linear RAG scanning mediates secondary Igk rearrangements and receptor editing.
- New et al. 2024: developmental checkpoint and heavy-chain editing example in glycan-reactive B cells.

## Less Central / Species Context

- Sinkora et al. 2022: comparative immunoglobulin rearrangement arrays across species.
- Pursell et al. 2024 preprint: bat IgH locus duplication and maintained allelic exclusion; useful as species-context only.
